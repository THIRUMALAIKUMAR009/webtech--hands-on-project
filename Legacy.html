Legacy

Dr. Kalam’s legacy as a leader, teacher, and visionary remains unparalleled. He inspired millions of young people to dream big and contributed to scientific advancements that positioned India as a strong player in space and defense technology. His books, especially "Wings of Fire," continue to motivate youth across the world.
