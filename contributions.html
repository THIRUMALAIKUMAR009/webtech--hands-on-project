Contributions

Dr. Kalam was instrumental in the development of India’s ballistic missile and space technology. He spearheaded projects such as the SLV-III, which successfully launched the Rohini satellite in 1980, and the Integrated Guided Missile Development Program (IGMDP), which led to the creation of the Agni and Prithvi missiles.
